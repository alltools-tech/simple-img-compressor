# About Simple Image Compressor Simple Image Compressor is designed to help users reduce image file sizes while maintaining high quality. Whether you're a web developer, designer, or someone looking to save storage space, our tool provides a simple and efficient way to compress images. ## Our Mission Our mission is to make image compression accessible and hassle-free for everyone. We believe in privacy, speed, and user-friendliness. ## Who Can Use This Tool? Anyone! From students to professionals, Simple Image Compressor is free for all.
